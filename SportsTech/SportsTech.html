Sports Technology Speculative Design

Speculative design and video creation as part of a PhD candidate's thesis.

External Link- kolovsam.com
Duration- Jan 2021 to June 2021, 20 weeks
Type- Group project
Context- Directed Research Group for a PhD candidate's thesis research project.
Skills- Speculative Design, Stakeholder Analysis, Persona,
Video creation (Storyboarding, Scripting, Video Editing)

Project Goal

To create a series of videos portraying speculative design of future sports technology, and specifically aiming to inspire discussion about values of privacy in terms of data tracking for student athletes.

Process Overview

For this project, I ideated 20+ possible future sports tracking technologies. As a group, we discussed and examined how personal values that could be strengthened or overuled by the technologies. For this discussion, we also looked at and analyzed interviews from student athletes, coaches, and athelete medical workers about both concerns and appreciations about tracking technology in the context of athletes.

My team picked one of our ideated technology, decided on what values to emphasize in our video, then created the video. My role includes writing, storyboarding, scripting, and editing the video.
